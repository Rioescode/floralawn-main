## How to Use These Templates

1. **Select the appropriate template** based on customer needs
2. **Replace placeholders** like [CUSTOMER_NAME], [PROPERTY_ADDRESS], etc. with actual information
3. **Customize pricing** based on your agreement
4. **Adjust terms** as needed for specific situations
5. **Send via email or SMS** using the contracts management page
6. **Print for signatures** if physical copies are needed

## Placeholder Guide

When filling out contracts, replace these placeholders:

- `[CUSTOMER_NAME]` - Customer's full name
- `[PROPERTY_ADDRESS]` - Street address
- `[CITY]` - City name
- `[PHONE]` - Phone number
- `[EMAIL]` - Email address
- `[PROPERTY_SIZE]` - Size (e.g., "1/2 acre", "Less than 1/4 acre")
- `[FREQUENCY]` - Service frequency (Weekly, Bi-Weekly, Monthly)
- `[DAY_OF_WEEK]` - Preferred service day
- `[START_DATE]` - Contract start date
- `[MONTHLY_PRICE]` - Monthly rate
- `[SPECIAL_INSTRUCTIONS]` - Any special notes or requirements

## Tips for Sending Contracts

1. **Always personalize** - Use actual customer information
2. **Be clear on pricing** - Make sure pricing is clearly stated
3. **Set expectations** - Explain what services are included
4. **Include contact info** - Make it easy for customers to reach you
5. **Follow up** - Check if customers have questions after receiving contract

## Contract Terms to Consider

- **Payment terms** - When payment is due
- **Cancellation policy** - How to cancel the contract
- **Service frequency** - How often services will be performed
- **Weather policy** - What happens in bad weather
- **Property access** - How contractors will access the property
- **Liability** - Who is responsible for what
- **Additional services** - How extras are handled
